Hello! My name is

Rashmi Mam

Business Analyst & Tutor at Dectac
At Dectec, we didn’t just build a BA course — we built a launchpad for real-world success. Backed by years of freelancing, client consulting, and live project delivery, we saw the gap between theory and reality — and teamed up to close it. Our mission? To shape confident, job-ready Business Analysts through hands-on practice, AI integration, stakeholder interaction, and proposal mastery. Whether you’re a fresher or a career switcher, we’re here to help you not just learn BA — but live it.

Learn like a student, grow like a pro, rise like a leader — that’s the Dectec way

As the Lead Business Analysis Mentor at Dectec, I bring a clear goal — to bridge the gap between theory and the real business world. With hands-on experience working across client projects, product teams, and freelance consulting, I’ve seen what it really takes to succeed as a Business Analyst today. From requirement gathering and stakeholder handling to writing SRS documents and using tools like ChatGPT, Lucidchart, and Miro — I’ve trained learners to master what the industry demands. My approach isn’t just about slides or checklists — it’s about giving you the confidence to speak with clients, handle ambiguity, and deliver with clarity. At Dectec, we believe that with structured mentoring and practice on real use-cases, anyone — regardless of background — can become a strong, job-ready BA.
